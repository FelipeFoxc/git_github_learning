About Us

A contract manufacturing (CM) company that manufactures electronic circuit boards for large OEMs (Original Equipment Manufacturers). The OEMs design the harware and circuits, manufacturing assemlby techniques which include automated assembly lines where machines place the parts on pre-printed circuit boards, send then trough ovens where the parts are soldered to the boards, and wash the boards after they are soldered. The process involves set ups of the placement machines for each board; programming the profiles of the "ovens" so the temperatures are appropiate for the boards. After the boards come off the lines, they are tested using Autoamted Test equipment. Failures are tracked and brought to an in-house Failure Analysis lab to determine why the parts are failing and to determine where in the process problems might be ocurring.
